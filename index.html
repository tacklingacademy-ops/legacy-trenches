Legacy in the Trenches

A Journey of Faith and Football

Thurmond Moore dedicated forty years to shaping men, not just players, exemplifying a commitment rooted in faith and purpose.

He coached NFL defensive lines for five teams. He trained 71 NFL players—including future Hall of Famers Aaron Donald and J.J. Watt. He created the Chest Pop Tackling System, reaching 50,000+ players worldwide.

But this isn't a coaching manual. It's a story about faithfulness. About four decades of showing up in obscurity and excellence. About investing in souls—from high school fields to NFL training rooms—and refusing to separate faith from football.

About the Author

THURMOND W. MOORE JR. spent over 40 years coaching NFL defensive linemen and linebackers for five teams (Cardinals, 49ers, Raiders, Rams, Chargers), as well as at UCLA, Syracuse, Cal/Berkeley, and NFL Europe.

He founded the Tackling Academy, created the Chest Pop Tackling System, and trained 71 NFL players, including Aaron Donald and J.J. Watt—reaching 50,000+ players worldwide.

Coach Moore currently serves as Defensive Coordinator at Bellarmine College Preparatory in San Jose, California, where he continues to shape young men through the principles of faith, discipline, and excellence.
